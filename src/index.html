About

Pints in the Sun addresses the very real problem of finding a pub that will be sunny in the evening, and not overshadowed by adjacent buidlings.

Using building outline data from Open Streetmap, the locality around a pub (located via Four Square) is rendered in 3D with three.js (map projection conversion couristy of the D3 library) and onto it, a directional light is projected; from the part of the sky where the sun would be at certain times of year/day. According to a Solar Almanac Calculation, helpfully implmented in JS by SunCalc

Hacked together at POKE, with: Open Street Map data, GeoJSON, D3, ThreeJS, SunCalc, FourSquare and maths
